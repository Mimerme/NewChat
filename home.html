Stubz is anonymous

Each stub is completely anonymous, no data
about our users are collected, sold, or given away.
Everything from the nicknames to the chat logs are all
purged when the room reaches its expiration

Stubz is free & open source

Stubz is a free service for everyone
and is open source on Github as well.

Stubz is convinent

Creating your own stub chatroom is just a few clicks away.
And joining a chat room just requires just a nickname and
the room name, no more accounts and user passwords.

What is Stubz?

Stubz is a chatroom service that focuses on
anonymity and convience. Unlike other chat system such
such Google Hangouts and Facebook Messenger,
each chatroom (or stub) is similar to an IRC
channel. Anyone is allowed to join, and joining
a stub is a simple as clicking on a URL.


Steps to creating a new stub

Step 1

Just click the create room button to start.
You will be redirected to a new page where you
can specify the options for your room

Step 2

Specify the room name, description, as
well as an optional password, and
declare the time in hours you want your
room to last. Don't worry, you can renew
the room for another 24 hours if you so please

Step 3

When you finally create your room you
will be redirected to the room you just
created. Specify the nickname an begin
chatting with those in the lobby
