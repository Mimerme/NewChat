Stubs

Stubs is a completly anonoymous chatting service that collects no
data on its users and is easy and conviniant. There are plans for it
to be feature packed with integration of additional applications as
well as compelete customization, but Stubs is only in alpha testing
and those features are just a few ways down the road.

How do you use Stubs?

1) Click 'New Room' and you will be redirected to a new page
2) Enter the desired room name and description then click 'Create Room'
3) Note that when you create your room you must choose how many hours you wish for the room to last
4) When the room is 2 hours from expiring you will be allowed to renew the room for another 24 hours
5) Now go communicate!
